What is Dash2Trade

Dash2.Trade is designed to take your crypto trading to the next level. From creating and testing trading strategies to tracking the latest news and on-chain data, Dash2.Trade makes you stay on top of the market.

Dash2Trade excels at revealing the data that has a true impact on performance , empowering you with a focused insight into the metrics which are most important when making wise trading decisions .

We have identified some of the most powerful points of data that allow the trade to formulate a holistic view of the market and make impactful trading decisions.

Examples of these data points include social statistics, developer activity, meme culture, price action, momentum, clear view of support and resistances and liquidity pools.

Aside from the traditional data points for technical analysis, our Dash2Trade platform also incorporates all these relevant points of data into a layout which makes it easy for traders and investors to conceptualise and understand.

DASH2 TRADE

  • BACKTESTER

    Dash2Trade’s backtester system will allow users to create a portfolio of various strategies based on Dash2Trade’s metrics and track their performance. User’s will be able to directly check whether their thinking and strategy will perform in the market. The backtester will also come with a metric to approximate the risk of a given strategy.

  • EVENT STREAMS

    One of the features we’re excited about are the event streams which will give a consistent feed of relevant information for tokens that users decide to follow. The event stream stream will be powered by an outlier detection algorithm that only shows events in the feed when they are relevant.

  • TECHNICAL INDICATORS

    Dash2Trade’s technical signals such as price correlations on assets order book insights, relative strength indicators and market momentum in real time can be used by traders to make entrances and exits on an asset.

  • ON-CHAIN METRICS

    Dash2Trade will feature fully on-chain statistics for token and wallet activities. The on-chain data will be fully aggregated and standardised into a standard internal system within the dashboard to enable decisions to be made on active trades.

  • SOCIAL METRICS

    Social metrics track how active discussions are for tokens, how widespread they are, and even how much the asset repository is getting updates pushed to it.

  • RISK PROFILER AND STRATEGY RECOMMENDER

    Dash2Trade is committed to helping our traders build the strategies that match their psychology and approach to the market and their risk to reward ( R:R ) appetite

  • COMMUNITY

    By creating a focused group of individuals who are dedicated to learning and improving their performance , our community will provide an edge for any trader who is part of it. The wisdom of many is a tried and tested concept and we believe that our community will provide the edge which is not available on any other platform.
